💞 Our Journey 💞

🌷 First Hello

Love, if you remember, it was the month of June, evening time here when I texted you first. I was hoping that you would reply, but I was unsure as I thought you were beyond my reach. And I was so shocked to find that you were from the Philippines, I literally stalked your profile and asked you how you have all Indian followers, it was kinda awkward and embarrassing. But we still became friends and started talking casually.

💬 Endless Talks

We started to talk in the morning and then in the evening. I also sent you photos of our group party, we even talked about attending each other's wedding, not knowing it would be our wedding. I remember sometimes Mummy would scold me for delaying dinner, but I really wanted to talk more. Then after I returned to college I tried to make some distance between us by not texting you. Instead it made me realise that I can’t spend a day without talking to you. You were really upset with me that time, I’m sorry for that. Since then we managed all ups and downs and talked everyday. I really used to get jealous whenever you talked about other boys and it was my first hint that I am falling for you, but I ignored and tried to keep things casual which of course didn’t work coz we were destined to be together and I feel so blessed about it.

💖 Becoming Us

You know I found that I have feelings for you on the day of Janmashtami, I was fasting that day and we talked the most that day. And finally I expressed myself on September 3rd and you said yes, it was such an unknown feeling I was literally crying but also I was so happy that I wanted to go on jumping all around. We have evolved since then and now it's our 2nd monthsary. You’ve become an integral part of my life, every time I think about you and keep on making plans. I have also hurt you many times but you forgive me and allow me to become better for you, I am truly grateful to you. I love you and hope you keep loving me the way you do. I miss you very much and soon I will come to meet you.

Made with love 💕 for the one who owns my heart — Akash
